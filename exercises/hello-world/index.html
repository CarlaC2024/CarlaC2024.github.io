How to Make Hney Muffins
  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in a small bowl
  2. Mix eggs, honey, oat milk, and vanilla in a seperate bowl
  3. Add the dry mixture to the liquid one in the two batches, mixing thoroughly
  4. Distribute mixture into greased muffin tins
  5. Bake for 16 minutes, or until lightly browned
  6. Enjoy!
