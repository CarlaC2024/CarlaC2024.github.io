Save The Orangutans

Mother orangutans spend a lot of time with their babies and infants. Young adult orangutans frequently socialize with other orangutans of the same age, but occasionally ‘visit’ their mothers until around 15 to 18 years old.

We are endangered

Both the endangered Bornean orangutan and the critically endangered Sumatran orangutan are in need of urgent help. It’s estimated that there are fewer than 7,500 Sumatran orangutans left in the wild, which makes the Rainforest Alliance’s Indonesian work in palm oil certification and carbon sequestration—both measures that are helping to protect orangutan habitat—vitally important.
